Scope of works

Design and construction of a 7MW industrial-scale smart microgrid energy system at Deakin’s Waurn Ponds campus in Geelong.

Results

Will be the largest solar system installed at an Australian University - a 14.5-hectare solar energy generation farm.

Reduce carbon emissions by 12,000 tonnes per year and generate half of the University’s energy needs on-site.

The microgrid can be expanded to include research of other technologies such as hydrogen storage that could lead to hydrogen and electric vehicle integration
